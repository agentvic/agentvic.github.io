## Conflict resolution for individuals. 

Sometimes we have internal conflicts that need to be resolved in order to move on to a new challenge. This is for adults over the age of 16. An internal conflict can manifest in many ways, and can limit growth if ignored or avoided.

## Interpersonal conflict resolution.

Interpersonal relationships can be tricky when underlying conflict goes unresolved. Conflict can show up in marriage, friendship, sibling relationships and the relationship between parent and child. 

## Corporate facilitation.

When working in a team setting there are many types of conflict that can arise and avoiding it can in the long run be detrimental to the culture of the company.  Working through the conflict as a team with a neutral party guiding the process, is a great way to get your team members on the same page, bring them closer together, and their productivity to increase as they can work together to be more effective, then before. A whole is greater than the sum of its parts, and what better way to become a whole than to resolve conflicts keeping them apart.
